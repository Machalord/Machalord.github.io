ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Matias, a passionate video game developer. Since I was a child, video games have been my greatest source of inspiration and entertainment, and now I have the privilege of contributing to this fascinating world as a programmer.

I am an eternal optimist, always looking for the positive side of things and trying to spread that energy to those around me. I firmly believe in the importance of living in the moment and making the most of every experience life has to offer.

In my portfolio, you'll find a collection of projects I've worked on, from experimental prototypes to full games. Each of them represents a chapter in my journey as a developer and I'm excited to share them with you.

If you're looking for a passionate and dedicated collaborator for your next video game project, I'm ready to dive headfirst into the challenge!

I'm always open to new connections and friendships, so don't hesitate to say hello. I would love to meet you!

Thanks for stopping by and I hope you enjoy exploring my work as much as I enjoyed creating it!
